Microcrypt Zoo

A Graph Visualization of the Relationships between Quantum Cryptographic Primitives in MicroCrypt

The graph on this website describes known connections between quantum cryptographic primitives. The emphasis is on MicroCrypt: primitves that are based on assumptions which are potentially wearker than one-way functions. Hovering with your mouse cursor over a connection will show a tooltip with reference to a work that investigates the relation between the two connected nodes. Clicking on the connection will take you to a website with more details on the respective work. Hovering over a node will show a tooltip with the name of the primitive and other. Nodes which share a color are equivalent. Contributions are welcome! See https://github.com/sattath/microcrypt-zoo for details.
